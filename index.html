How to be an in demand Programmer/Software Developer in 2016

One of the hottest job markets of 2016 is in Web Development. Millions of people throughout the world connect to each other, family, friends, schools, companies, governments through Web Sites. People expect an engaging experience with the Web and this can only be brought about by Web Developers. Companies understand this and hire Web Developers to create the best possible experience Users will have when they visit and engage with their websites. With the ever-growing amount of websites and maintenance of those websites there comes an ever-growing demand for Web Developers.

Demand

A recent quote from “The Creative Group” states: “These masters of the web don’t just write code; they make the digital magic happen. They’re responsible for what you experience every time you open a browser, app or online game – from content to presentation to behavior layers. So it comes as no surprise that these professionals made our list of hot jobs for 2016.”

One of the largest growth areas is in eCommerce (think Amazon.com as a hot example) as online purchasing is growing faster than bricks and mortar retail.

The U.S. Bureau of Statistics has published data that points to a 27% growth for Web Developers in the next eight years.
