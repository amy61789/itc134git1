Skills & Education

Experience

  • Work Experience 2-5+ years
  • GitHub Account or a portfolio to display projects worked on.

People/Social Skills

  • Excellent communication
  • Able to work within teams
  • Flexibility
  • Ability to meet deadlines

Technical Skills

  • Experience Developing Software
  • Be dedicated to high quality code and devoted to customer experience.
  • Understand how to strike the balance between scalable/maintainable code.
  • See potential scalability or performance problems before they happen.
  • Adopt industry standards and team-specific coding practices.

Education

  • Coding Bootcamps
  • Programming/Web Development Certificate
  • Associates Degree
  • Bachelors
